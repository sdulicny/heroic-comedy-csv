What is Comedy Against Authority?

Comedy Against Authority was coined by Dr. Diana Solomon and Dr. Sean Zwagerman. This digital archive features social media content that uses comedy as a way to fight against societal and political authority, to shed light on the importance of comedy as an anti-authoritarian tool in contemporary society.
